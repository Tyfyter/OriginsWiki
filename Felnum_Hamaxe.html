Felnum Hamaxe

The Felnum Hamaxe is a pre-Hardmode hamaxe that is crafted using Felnum Bars. Like all hamaxes, it can used to break walls and slope tiles or used to chop and shake trees. It can also act as a weapon able to be thrown by pressing the Interact button and inflicts Static Shock for 2-3.5 seconds.
