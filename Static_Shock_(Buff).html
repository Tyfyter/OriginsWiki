Static Shock

Static Shock is a debuff that is applied by some Felnum-themed items as a pre-Hardmode version of the Electrified debuff. When applied to an NPC, they will receive and shock any nearby enemies around them for 3 damage each second. Defiled enemies or enemies simultaneously inflicted with the Wet debuff have their shockchain distance and damage received doubled. Defiled enemies inflicted with Wet and Static Shock instead have the shockchain distance and damage received tripled. When applied to a player, the player will lose 3 life per second.
