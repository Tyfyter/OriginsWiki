Soulhide Armor

Soulhide armor is an armor set consisting of the Soulhide Mask, Soulhide Coat and Soulhide Guards. In order to craft the full set the player will need 42 Demonite Ore and 66 Rotten Chunk.

A full set of Soulhide armor provides the following benefits:

- +10 defense
- +10% melee weapon size
- +12% melee attack speed
- +14% knockback
Set bonus
- Enemies within 10 tiles of the player are inflicted with Shadowflame
- Enemies within 15 tiles of the player are inflicted with Weak
