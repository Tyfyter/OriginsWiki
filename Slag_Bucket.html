Slag Bucket

The Slag Bucket is a Hardmode shield accessory combining the Scrap Barrier with the Shield of Retribution. The shield provides an immunity to knockback, Burning and Impeding Shrapnel. Additionally, it reflects all of the damage the player recieves to 3 of the nearest enemies. The projectiles released by the Slag Bucket after taking damage inflict On Fire! and the player recieves a net gain life regeneration while burning themselves.

The cursed version additionally provides a 40% damage reduction while above 80% of total health. It also applies a 4-second buffer before life regeneration occurs (even from other accessories) after taking damage while above 50% of total health.

The normal version instead applies 3 additional Defense, a 15% damage reduction and a life regeneration boost.
