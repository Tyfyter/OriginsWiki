Splitsplash

The Splitsplash is a pre-Hardmode magic weapon that has a 1/5 (20%) chance to be dropped by destroyed Gel Blisters in the Riven Hive. The Splitsplash fires a burst of 3 amoeba bubbles that split into 2 smaller bubbles.

The best modifier is Mythical.
