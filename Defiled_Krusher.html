Defiled Krusher

The Defiled Krusher is a pre-Hardmode enemy that spawns in the Defiled Wastelands. The Defiled Krusher very slowly approaches the player, regenerates life and releases a Defiled Wisp upon death in Master Mode like any other Defiled enemy. It is stronger than the Defiled Cyclops at the cost of its speed.

Notes

- Even though it appears to be 4 tiles tall, it can fit through 3-tile spaces similarly to the Face Monster.

Trivia
