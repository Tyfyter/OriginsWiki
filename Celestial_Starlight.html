Celestial Starlight

[statblock Celestial_Starlight]

The Celestial Starlight is a pre-Hardmode accessory crafted using a Celestial Magnet and Dim Starlight. Equipping it increases the pickup range for Stars and causes them to generate from critical hits. It also provides a source of light brighter than its original.

Tips

- The Celestial Starlight is very beneficial when the player reforges their equipment and weapons around critical strike chance, so mana stars can fall as often as possible.
- The best weapon to use with the Celestial Starlight is the Laser Tag Gun for its 50% chance for critical hits.
