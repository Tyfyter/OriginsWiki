Ace Shrapnel

The Ace Shrapnel is a pre-Hardmode launcher that is obtained from the Broken Record or crafted using NE-8 and Scrap. The Ace Shrapnel fires a constant stream of frenzied shrapnel, in a manner somewhat similar to the Flamethrower, that inflict Impeding Shrapnel for 5 seconds. It requires Scrap as ammunition to fire this weapon.

The best modifier is Heavy-Duty for its damage boost, such that enemies afflicted by Impeding Shrapnel can spread the effect faster.

Trivia

- The Ace Shrapnel is inspired by a weapon of the same name from the Virexian mobile game.
