Forbidden Voice

The Forbidden Voice is a pre-Hardmode accessory dropped by the Eater of Worlds in Master Mode. While the cursed version is equipped, the player is Silenced but can use a mapped keybind in the game controls menu to silence any enemies within 25 tiles of the player, preventing them from firing projectiles. As the player doesn't use this ability, they will accrue up to 10% damage reduction overtime that remains until the player uses its ability again.

While the normal version is equipped, it just increases melee weapon size by 25% and melee weapon damage by 10%. Furthermore, all attacks inflict the Weak debuff for 3 seconds.

Tips

- The player is always affected by the Cursed debuff while the cursed version of Forbidden Voice is equipped, but its duration timer below the debuff's icon is used to display Forbidden Voice's ability cooldown. Whenever the timer reaches 0 seconds, Forbidden Voice's ability can be used again.
