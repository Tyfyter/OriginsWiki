Forbidden Voice

The Forbidden Voice is a pre-Hardmode accessory dropped by the Eater of Worlds in Master Mode. While the cursed version is equipped, the player is Silenced, but the mouth will shout at enemies dealing considerable damage and knockback. While the normal version is equipped, just allows the player to spit at enemies inflicting Weak and moderate damage. Both versions use up the player's mana, but the cursed version using up more.

Forbidden Voice is a lore item indicated by "You hear whispers nearby..."
