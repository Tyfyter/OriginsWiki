Sleetfire

The Sleetfire is a pre-Hardmode ranged weapon that can be crafted with Ice Blocks, an Ice Torch and Shiverthorn. It is a short-ranged flamethrower that looks as if it is throwing sleet-like fire. The flames from this weapon inflict Frostburn for 5-10 seconds.

Its best modifier is Heavy Caliber.
