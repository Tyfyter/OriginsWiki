Biomes

If you are looking for the official page of vanilla biomes, click here.

Biomes are the different types of areas that any Terraria world can contain. Every biome has its own characteristic terrain blocks, walls, collectible items, backgrounds, enemies, critters, music themes, Angler quest fish and other features.

The Ashen, Defiled and Riven are all "contagious" biomes which spread to certain adjacent tiles. In pre-Hardmode, the evil biomes will only overtake other biomes via grasses, ice, sand and stone, but once Hardmode begins all three biomes can spread directly through most soils.

Space biomes

The Dawn

The Dawn is a planned post-Mech sky biome of holy themes. The Dawn is a sacred place that has physically manifested into the Terraria world so the angels that watch after it can handle the current instability of the world more directly. Every entity that is not holy enough shall not enter the confines of the Dawn, and will be met with "undesirable consequences" in the event that were to happen. The Dawn is here for no other purpose than to safeguard Terraria.

Surface biomes

Ashen Factory

The Ashen Factory is a planned third world evil added by Origins. The Factory is a machine that has created a robotic society beyond its intended purpose. Not much is known as to how the Factory arrived to the Terraria world, but it is known to be a serious threat to life on the planet due to its pollution-generating nature. The Ashen does not spread unlike the other four evils, but it can only be removed through demolition. As a matter of fact, it offers the most content for Demolitionists.

Brine Pool

The Brine Pool is a natural hardmode mini-biome deep within the Jungle. It is a hostile environment and hard to enter due to its brine-rich waters, which will overwhelm any unprotected entities that dare swim its waters. Ironically, it harbors a very diverse ecosystem based around the hydrothermal vents within its hollow interior. It would be of the Terrarian's interest to explore this biome early into hardmode, with proper protection of course.

Defiled Wastelands

The Defiled Wastelands is the first world evil added by Origins. The Defiled is an ancient entity that was banished to the Dusk by the Dryad and her kin during its infancy due to the forseen consequences of letting it live. It was released from the Dusk after the Moon Lord attacked the inhabitants of Terraria and has been rooting itself into the world ever since. It recognizes the Terrarian and attempts to counter them with its anti-bodies, constantly improving them and introducing new forms in hopes of stopping the Terrarian in their tracks. It is an intelligent entity and it offers a unique, story-driven progression of its own. The Defiled offers a lot of content to Mages.

Eutrophic Sea

The Eutrophic Sea is a unique archetype of the Riven Hive that has encompassed an Ocean. The Eutrophic Sea is a horrifying sight to behold on the lands of Terraria as the Riven Hive is predominantly full of sub-aquatic species for when it gets flooded. When the Riven is able to reach an ocean, it spreads fast due to the salt and mass-rich water, not only making it easier for it to synthesize Alkahest, but also providing it with more mass to use. Even if you made a boat of glass, the viscosity of the gel wouldn't let you go anywhere.

Riven Hive

The Riven Hive is the second world evil added by Origins. Don't let its beauty fool you for it is very easy to become one with the Hive's ecosystem. At base level, the Riven Hive is just like any other environment, except that it was molded by an entity known as the "Primordial Amoeba". The Amoeba isn't inherently evil, but it is sentient and rather selfish, forcing its hand, or rather flagella, to the world around it. The Riven Hive is a hostile biome where certain species have evolved and even been born directly from its hot and humid caverns. The Riven offers the most content for Summoners out of the five evils.

Cavern biomes

Defiled Caverns

The Defiled Caverns is created in the Cavern layer upon defeating the Wall of Flesh. The presence of the Defiled Caverns is a terrible sign that the Defiled is making rapid progress to consuming the entire world and must be stopped immediately. Like the other underground evil biomes, it is one of the only places to obtain Souls of Night, but not its evil substance: Black Bile. Uniquely, the Defiled Caverns' theme can be heard in pre-hardmode at the appropriate depth.

Fiberglass Undergrowth

The Fiberglass Undergrowth is a natural Cavern biome located directly under the Brine Pool in the Underground Jungle. The Undergrowth is full of enchanted elemental spawn that safeguard the biome from any intruders. Deeper within, however, resides the colossal Fiberglass Weaver spider that will also protect its home.

Riven Caverns

The Riven Caverns form in the Cavern layer upon the Wall of Flesh's defeat. The Riven Caverns is no different than the Riven Hive itself, merely a sign that it has spread quite fast. Don't be too hasty, however, as the layout of the caverns make the Riven's gooey amebic gel even more dangerous, and Mitoworms multiply rampantly as well.

Hell biomes

The Dusk

The Dusk is a hardmode Hell biome that serves as a purgatory for Terraria's worst offenders. Contrary to the underworld surrounding it, the Dusk is actually of lukewarm temperature making it more hospitable but this is because of the illuminant vortex at its center. The Dusk is bastioned by the clockwork machinations built to watch after the criminal denizens banished to its confines, and, send those shall attempt escape through the aforementioned vortex. The Dusk is ultimately the other safeguard established by the world itself to maintain it like its holy-equivalent, the Dawn.

The Cosmos biomes

Fractured Isles

The Fractured Isles are a planned post-Moon Lord biome residing within the Cosmos dimension. The Isles were once a part of a planet home to an alien civilization that collapsed from an unknown cause. There are artificial structures on the the isles that remain the test of time to serve as some kind of ceremonial grounds for their long lost and destined warriors.

Structures

Cubekon Temple

The Cubekon Temple is a post-Moon Lord temple-like structure generally found adjacent to Marble Caves. The Temple is very blocky and modular by design despite being carved out of Batholith. Within the temple, resides one of the most technologically-advanced species known to Terraria and they don't welcome any intruders. The temple is only accessible to those of its own kind, measured by the amount of energy within their vessels, but that ultimately means its accessible to any entity able to wield such power, like the Terrarian. The Cubekons are always curious to learn more, but currently have been working towards obtaining their own version of the singularity.
