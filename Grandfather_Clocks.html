Grandfather Clocks

If you are looking for the official page of vanilla grandfather clocks, click here.

Grandfather Clocks are functional furniture items which provide information about the in-game time. If a player is nearby a Grandfather Clock (about 80 tiles horizontally and 50 tiles vertically), the time is displayed on the right side of the screen, underneath the minimap.
