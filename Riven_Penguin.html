Riven Penguin

The Riven Penguin is an enemy derived from a Penguin that has been corrupted, either by the player using Sentient Powder or by a Blood Moon event on a Riven world. The Riven Penguin behaves like its vanilla counterparts: the Corrupt Penguin and Vicious Penguin.

Notes

- Although they spawn during the Blood Moon event, they cannot drop Bloody Tears.
