Beds

If you are looking for the official page of vanilla beds, click here.

Beds are functional furniture items which allow the player to set a new spawn point by pressing the Open/Activate button on the foot half of the bed. This requires it to be placed in a mostly valid house. Beds furthermore allow the player to sleep in them by pressing the Open/Activate button on the head half of the bed. This makes time pass five times faster and gives a boost to health regeneration, similar to chairs and sofas. Beds fulfill the comfort item requirement for purposes of NPC housing.
