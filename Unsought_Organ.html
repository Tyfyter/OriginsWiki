Unsought Organ

The Unsought Organ is a Hardmode accessory crafted using an Olid Organ and Razorwire. When equipped, half of all damage the player recieves is replicated across three enemies within 15 tiles from the player whilst also being inflicted with Toxic Shock. Additionally, damage and critical strike chance are increased by 5%, all attacks inflict Toxic Shock and enemy aggression is reduced by 275.
