Amber of Embers

The Amber of Embers is a Hardmode magic weapon that is crafted using Amber, Carburite and Valkyrum Bars at a Hardmode Anvil. Using it fires a wave of fire that travels 60 tiles and can pierce tiles alongside 4 targets. When hit, there is a 50% chance to apply Hellfire for 5 seconds.

Its best modifier is Mythical.
