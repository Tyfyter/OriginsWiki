Dragon's Breath

The Dragon's Breath is a Hardmode ranged weapon found in Shadow Chests in the Underworld. It has a longer range than most flamethrowers and inflicts the Hellfire debuff for 3 seconds. It uses Fireblossoms as ammo (similarly to the Boiler and Firespit) and the flames it throws are more likely to spiral the further they go.

Its best modifier is Unreal.
