Grapple Ravel

Grapple Ravel is a Hardmode accessory that is crafted using Ravel and the Super Turbo Reel. Grapple Ravel turns the player into a small sphere, upon double-tapping the Down button, that provides slightly more speed than their normal form and gives a 10% chance to dodge attacks. Grapple Ravel also allows the player to utilize their equipped hook.
