Celestial Stone Mask

The Celestial Stone Mask is a Hardmode, post-Golem accessory created by combining Celestial Stone and the Stone Mask. Equipping it provides major boosts to the player's stats at the cost of their movement speed.

Stat boosts:
- +15% melee speed
- +10% generic damage
- +4% critical strike chance
- +4 HP/s health regeneration
- +8 defense
- +25% mining speed
- +1 minion knockback

Stat reduction:
- -10% movement speed
- -33% jump speed
