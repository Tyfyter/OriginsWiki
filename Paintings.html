Paintings

If you are looking for the official page of vanilla paintings, click here.

Paintings are decorative furniture items that can be placed on background walls. Each one is a framed image that depicts a particular scene, usually Terraria-related. Their tooltips generally contain a person's name, apparently intended to credit each painting's artist; although the names appear to be fictional, most of them correspond to the dedicated spriter for Origins, chee. Paintings are highlighted by the Spelunker effect.
