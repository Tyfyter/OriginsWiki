Defiled Phantom

The Defiled Phantom is a pre-Hardmode enemy that spawns in the Defiled Wastelands. The Defiled Phantom will glide towards the player in spurts, regenerate life and release a Defiled Wisp upon death in Master Mode like any other Defiled enemy.
