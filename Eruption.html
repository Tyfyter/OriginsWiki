Eruption

The Eruption is a pre-Hardmode launcher crafted using Hellstone Bars. It converts its ammunition into miniature geysers, which remain in place until stepped on by an enemy, releasing hot magma upwards into a narrow row and inflicting the On Fire! debuff.

Its best modifier is Guileless.
