Entangled Energy

Entangled Energy is a pre-Hardmode accessory that is obtained from the Treasure Bag dropped by the Fiberglass Weaver in Expert Mode. While equipped, Fiberglass weapons will gain a flat increase to their damage based on the player's health and the ability to lifesteal. The rate at which the damage of Fiberglass weapons is increased can be expressed by "(Defense / 2) + Weapon damage".
