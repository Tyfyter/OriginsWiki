Etherealizer

The Etherealizer is a Hardmode Dungeon enemy that spawns close to the player. The Etherealizer will behave similarly to other Dungeon magic-casters like the Necromancer except that the magic it casts acts like the Magic Missile and tracks the player down.

Trivia

- The Bestiary entry for the Etherealizer: "She doesn't look it, but she's actually a really bad joker. Her tagline: "Somethings are best left ethrealized!""
