Toilets

If you are looking for the official page of vanilla toilets, click here.

Toilets are decorative furniture items that fulfill a house's comfort item requirement. In combination with a Work Bench or a Table, they also form minor crafting stations. Players can sit in toilets by using the Open/Activate button. NPCs can sit on toilets too.
