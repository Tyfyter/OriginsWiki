Abyssal Anchor

The Abyssal Anchor is a Hardmode, post-Golem accessory that is crafted with the Binding Book and Celestial Stone Mask. Equipping it provides major boosts to the player's stats at the cost of their movement speed.

Stat boosts:
- +15% melee speed
- +10% generic damage
- +5% magic damage
- +4% critical strike chance
- +4 HP/s health regeneration
- +20 mana capacity
- +8 defense
- +5% damage reduction
- +25% mana shielding
- +25% mining speed
- +1 minion knockback
- Greatly increased pickup range for Stars
- +130% fall speed

Stat reduction:
- -25% movement speed
- -40% jump speed

Notes

- Abyssal Anchor's effect is stackable with other mana shielding boosts (see item synergies).
- Mana shielding takes into account the damage the player would have received with 0 defense and 0% damage reduction. This results in the proper amount of damage reduced from the attack, but an inefficiency in the mana consumed from it.
- Whenever the player benefits from mana shielding, Mana Buffer will be applied preventing any mana regeneration for 3.2 seconds.
- The Binding Book does not stack with Magic Cuffs or any of its variants for decreased mana consumption.
