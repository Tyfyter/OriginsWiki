Banners

If you are looking for the official page of vanilla banners, click here.

Banners are functional furniture items that can be placed on the underside of blocks. Most enemies drop a banner based around them that can apply a buff to any players within a 170x125-tile area. The buff grants players +50% (+100% in Expert Mode) damage to the corresponding enemy and negate damage received by said enemy by 25% (50% in Expert Mode). Multiple of the same banner will not grant a stacked bonuses, but multiple different banners in the same vicinity will all take effect and consolidate into a single buff icon.
