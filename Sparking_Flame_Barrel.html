Sparking Flame Barrel

The Sparking Flame Barrel is a pre-Hardmode accessory that is crafted combining the Danger Barrel and Lightning Ring. When equipped, all explosives inflict On Fire! for 2-6 seconds and have their fuse time reduced by 33%, all attacks from the player inflict Static Shock for 3 seconds, and increases the shock chain range of Static Shock when using item that already inflict it.
