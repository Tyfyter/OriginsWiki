Matrix

The Matrix is a pre-Hardmode launcher that can be crafted using NE-8 and Sanguinite Bars. The Matrix converts its ammunition into 3-5 hovering nodes, similarly to the Bombardment, that connect to each other through a laser beam. When an enemy has entered the shape crated by the nodes, the area within the shape will explode.

Its best modifier is Guileless.
