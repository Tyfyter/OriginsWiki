Depth Ravel

Depth Ravel is a Hardmode accessory that is crafted using the Arctic Diving Gear, a Flipper and Ravel. Depth Ravel turns the player into a small sphere, upon double-tapping the Down button, that provides slightly more speed than their normal form and gives a 10% chance to dodge attacks. Depth Ravel allows seamless movement and swimming while submerged, gives the player another 18 seconds of breath, and causes the player to generate light while ravelled.
