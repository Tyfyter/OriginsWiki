Protomind

The Protomind is a Hardmode accessory combining a Brain of Confusion with the Potato Battery. When equipped, enemies have a 3/5 (60%) chance of receiving the Confused debuff for a minimum of 1.5 seconds. The chance, duration, and area of effect of the debuff are based on the amount of damage taken. Additionally, all magic projectiles home towards enemies.

Furthermore, the player wielding it has a 1/6 (16.67%) chance of dodging an attack, which inflicts the Cerebral Mindtrick debuff on them for 4 seconds. Despite being a debuff, it increases critical strike chance and summon damage by 10%. While the debuff is active, the player cannot dodge another attack.
