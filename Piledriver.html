Piledriver

The Piledriver is a pre-Hardmode pickaxe that is crafted using Sanguinite Bars and NE-8. Like all pickaxes, it is used to destroy tiles, but can also be used as a weapon and features a powerful charge attack by pressing and holding the Inspect button. This alternate use function can also be used to dig a bigger area when mining as well.
