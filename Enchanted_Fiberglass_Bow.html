Enchanted Fiberglass Bow

The Enchanted Fiberglass Bow is a pre-Hardmode enemy that spawns in the Fiberglass Undergrowth. It will remain stationary until it shoots at the player or receives damage. It will continue to teleport around the player until it is destroyed.

Notes

- The Enchanted Fiberglass Bow's teleportation can be countered by Unmisfire, allowing you to still hit it even if it teleports.
