Barnacle Bunny

The Barnacle Bunny is an enemy that has been corrupted, either by the player using Sentient Powder or by a Blood Moon event on a Riven world. The Barnacle Bunny behaves like its vanilla counterparts: the Corrupt Bunny and Vicious Bunny.

Notes

- Barnacle Bunnies can be farmed for the Bunny Hood and Barnacle Bunny Banner using a Bunny Statue, Pressure Plate, Sentient Powder and Wire. However, these statue-spawned ones do not drop coins.
- Although they spawn during the Blood Moon event, they cannot drop Bloody Tears.
