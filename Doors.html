Doors

If you are looking for the official page of vanilla doors, click here.

Doors are functional furniture items that prevent enemies from entering an area when closed. Players can open them by pressing the Open/Activate buttons on them. Doors can be placed in any space so long as it is exactly three blocks high, with ceiling and floor blocks. Friendly NPCs will also open and close doors during the day as they wander about.
