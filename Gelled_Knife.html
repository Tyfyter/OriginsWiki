Gelled Knife

The Gelled Knife is a consumable ranged weapon that is crafted using Throwing Knives and Sentient Powder. Each knife has a 1/2 (50%) chance to inflict Torn that increases in severity depending on how many consecutive times the targeted enemy has been struck by a Gelled Knife. They otherwise behave exactly like the Throwing Knives used to create them, except for having slightly better statistics overall, along with auto-use.
