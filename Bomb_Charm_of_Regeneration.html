Bomb Charm of Regeneration

The Bomb Charm of Regeneration is a pre-Hardmode accessory combining the Band of Regeneration and Bomb Charm. While equipped, all self-damage the player recieves is reduced by 15% and life regeneration is increased by 1 health every second. Additionally when the player receives any self-damage while this is equipped, they will regenerate 20% of the source's damage back for a short while after.
