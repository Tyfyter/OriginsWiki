Pincushion

The Pincushion is a pre-Hardmode tool sold by the Clothier NPC for [price 10|0|0]. It prevents tile destruction from explosives. While in the inventory, the Pincushion will prevent the destruction of all tiles from explosives. Pressing Open/Activate on the Pincushion in the player's inventory will toggle it between the Pincushion and Pinless Cushion, which also re-enables tile destruction from explosives. The player can press Open/Activate again to toggle the Pinless Cushion back into the Pincushion thereby toggling the effect.
