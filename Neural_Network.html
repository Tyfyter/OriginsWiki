Neural Network

The Neural Network is a pre-Hardmode gun that is currently obtained through the Broken Record or craftable using Sanguinite Bars and NE-8. The Neural Network inflicts a buff to the player that causes its fire rate to increase after every consecutive hit. The buff will disappear after the player misses 4 of their shots in a row or when swapping to another weapon. Its icon can also be right-clicked in order to remove it, if the player desires to for whatever reason. Neural Network's base damage is lowered for every stack of the buff in order to offset its fire rate, however, it will always deal a higher overall DPS as its fire rate increases.

In order to ensure the best accuracy, the best modifier is Hasty for offering the best improvement to weapon velocity.
