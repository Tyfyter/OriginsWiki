I-WTPA Standard

[statblock IWTPA_Standard]

The I-WTPA Standard is a pre-Hardmode accessory that has a 1/4 (25%) chance of being dropped by Undead Miners found Underground. It is also sold by the Demolitionist NPC for [price 5|0|0] after selling him 35 Peat Moss. While equipped, all explosive fuse times will be reduced by 33%.
