Sofas

If you are looking for the official page of vanilla sofas, click here.

Sofas are vanity furniture items that fulfill a house's comfort item requirement. Players can sit in sofas by using the Open/Activate button on them and can receive a boost in health regeneration, similar to beds and Sofas. Players can choose to sit on the left, middle or right positions, and thus up to 3 players can sit on a sofa at a time.
