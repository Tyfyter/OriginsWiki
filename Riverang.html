Riverang

The Riverang is a boomerang that is crafted with Encrusted Bars and Riven Carapace, or dropped by Gel Blisters for 1/6 (16.67%) chance. The Riverang homes to enemies that are close to it while in flight and it is undisturbed when moving in liquids.

Its best modifier is Godly or Demonic.
