Ocotoral Bud

The Ocotoral Bud is a pre-Hardmode summon weapon that can be obtained by fishing in the Riven Hive. It summons a barnacle turret sentry that can move with limitations. The turret cannot jump and cannot climb over tiles, but can freely move across a flat surface. It slowly shoots blobs of amebic gel at enemies, inflicting Torn for 2 seconds at a severity of 50%.

Ocotoral Bud's best modifier is Mythical.
