Astoxo

The Astoxo is a Hardmode bow that is crafted using a Daedalus Stormbow and Valkryum Bars. When an arrow fired from the Astoxo collides with a surface, a bolt of lightning is summoned where the arrow strikes. The lightning deals damage to every enemy above the struck location. The Astoxo gets a 50% boost to damage bonuses.

Its best modifier is Unreal.
