Alkaline Grenade

The Alkaline Grenade is a type of Hardmode explosive that can be crafted using Grenades and Alkaliphiliac Tissue or purchased from the Demolitionist NPC after selling 180 Peat Moss to him once Hardmode is reached. The alkaline Grenade explodes into 3-5 brine droplets that each inflict Toxic Shock lasting anywhere from 4.5 to 6 seconds.
