Brine Pool

"No brew I know rivals the Brine. Maybe I should steal some later." - The Witch Doctor

The Brine Pool is an aquatic Hardmode biome that features a diversity of enemies fitting in with its extremophiliac theme. The Brine Pool always spawns within a Jungle biome, but not always with a clear entrance from the top. It can be spotted from the presence of Dolomite and an opening on its top so that the player can enter. Within its shell, the Brine Pool is structured with open cells full of water that the player will have to swim through in order to explore deeper within.

While the player is inflicted with Toxic Shock while submerged in water nearby the Brine Pool or any other source, they will be unable to replenish their breath until no longer recieving Toxic Shock and out of water. Furthermore, Dolomite is impervious to any explosives. Because of these factors, proper protection against Toxic Shock and preparation for water travel is advised before navigating the Brine Pool.

Generation

The Brine Pool generates as a large oval-like shape with smaller cells within. Each of the cells connect with an adjacent cell aside from a few which may remain isolated from the rest. Some cells also connect to the top entrance, which is usually 12-20 tiles wide. Mud-grown Peat Moss encompass the inner regions of each cell, and Eitrite Ore will generate in veins within the Dolomite shell. Additionally, Brineglow vines will generate from the ceiling of each cell and illuminate a small area around them. Some veins of Mildew will appear within Dolomite as well, and spread along it serving as a miniature world evil for the Brine Pool. It can be cleansed using Purification Powder. Lastly, Geyser Cores will generate in Dolomite extensions within some cells to emit hydrothermal waste.

The Fiberglass Undergrowth also always spawns directly underneath the Brine Pool.

Trivia

- The Brine Pool was originally planned to be a large drop off not too dissimilar to its current entrance and have a brine cloud overlay over the player's screen, which would then inflict Toxic Shock. It also had a separate section off to the right that would lead to a temple-like area called "The Ruins", which has been removed for another mod idea later down the line.
