Abrasion Blaster

The Abrasion Blaster is a pre-Hardmode launcher that is crafted using Biocomponent 10 and Scrap. The Abrasion Blaster fires charged energy blasts with their explosive properties dependent on how long the player holds the charge. Additionally, Scrap will be removed from the player's inventory as the player holds the charge for a maximum of 3 seconds. The Abrasion Blaster will inflict self-damage for the final 5 charges and destroy tiles in the last 2.

The best modifier is Guileless.
