Amoeba Bugger

The Amoeba Bugger is a Hardmode enemy that is directly spawned from Barnacle Mounds in groups of 4-7 at a time. Amoeba Buggers fly and swarm the player with their great numbers and slow down the player from destroy their source Barnacle Mound. Amoeba Buggers drop nothing and therefore cannot be farmed.

Notes

- Amoeba Buggers only take up a quarter of a slot when counting towards the entity limit in a world, allowing four-times more the Amoeba Buggers to be present on screen than any other enemy.
