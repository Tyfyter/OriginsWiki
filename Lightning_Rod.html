Lightning Rod

The Lightning Rod is a pre-Hardmode fishing pole that is crafted using Felnum Bars. Like all fishing poles, it is used with bait for fishing, but can also be used as a weapon as it inflicts Static Shock for 1.5-2 seconds, and deals damage and pulls enemies towards the player when reeled in. It is also an effective fishing pole as its bobber has an instant travel time.
