Pustule Jellyfish

[statblock Pustule_Jellyfish]

The Pustule Jellyfish is an enemy that commonly appears in the Eutrophic Sea. It behaves similarly to normal Jellyfish, except that it will now kamikaze into the player to explode and deal significant damage to the player. It will turn into an alternate version of itself after exploding and continue to pursue the player, but cannot explode its pustule anymore.

Trivia

- The Bestiary entry for the Pustule Jellyfish: "A jellyfish that has adapted to the Riven Hive. The pustule is delicate and explodes whenever it needs to defend itself."
