Chairs

If you are looking for the official page of vanilla chairs, click here.

Chairs are decorative furniture items that fulfill a house's comfort item requirement. In combination with a Work Bench or a Table, they also form minor crafting stations. Players can sit in chairs by using the Open/Activate button on them and can receive a boost in health regeneration similar to beds and sofas. NPCs can sit on chairs too.
