Felnum Guardian

The Felnum Guardian is a pre-Hardmode enemy that spawns on the Floating Islands in Space after Skeletron has been defeated. The Guardian will approach the player and charge up for an electrical beam that inflicts Static Shock for 4-5 seconds. Like the Felnum Einheri, it will attack other non-Felnum-associated enemies.

Trivia

- The Bestiary entry for the Felnum Guardian: "A sentinel manifested by the felnum itself. It indiscriminately attacks anything that gets too close to felnum deposits."
