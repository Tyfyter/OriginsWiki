Handy Helper

The Handy Helper is a Hardmode accessory combining the Power Glove with the Amebic Vial. While equipped, gloved amebic tentacles will protect the player from projectiles and push away enemies that get too close. Additionally, the Handy Helper doubles melee knockback, increases melee speed by 12%, enables auto-swing on all melee weapons and whips, and increases the size of all melee weapons by 10%. Similarly to Amebic Vial, there is a unique visual effect on the player that can be turned off while equipped on the player.

The tentacles have a range of 4.4 tiles (71 units) at which they will protect the player and have a cooldown of 1.67 seconds before they are deployed again.
