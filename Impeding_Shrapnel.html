Impeding Shrapnel

Impeding Shrapnel is a debuff that is applied by most items and enemies found in the Ashen Factory. When applied to an NPC that is slain afterwards, the enemy will release 5-7 frenzied shrapnel where it died that also inflict Impeding Shrapnel and deal damage. When applied to a player, they will instead be instantly killed if at or below 20% of their max life, however, if the player is above that threshold they will instead be inflicted with Bleeding.
