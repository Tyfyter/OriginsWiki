Bleeding Obsidian Shard

Bleeding Obsidian Shards are a material that are harvested in amounts of 4-7 from mining Bleeding Obsidian. It is primarily used to craft Bleeding Obsidian blocks, which are then used to craft many Dusk-themed items. It is also used to craft Eitrite Bars.
