Waning Crescent

The Waning Crescent is a pre-Hardmode boomerang that is crafted combining Any Evil Boomerang, the Flamarang, Thorn Chakram and Trimarang. When thrown it will leave shadowy after-images of itself as it approaches its maximum range that can infinitely pierce enemies and damage them upon contact.

Its best modifier is Godly or Demonic.
