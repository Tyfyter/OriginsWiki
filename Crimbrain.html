Crimbrain

The Crimbrain is a pre-Hardmode enemy that spawns in the Crimson. The Crimbrain will fly towards the player with brief periods where it will circle back to attack again. When the Crimbrain hits the player it will also inflict Crimson Assimilation and Confused for 0.33 seconds.

Trivia

- The Bestiary entry for the Crimbrain: "These entities possess a higher standing within the Crimson heirarchy as they bear the knowledge of its secrets."
