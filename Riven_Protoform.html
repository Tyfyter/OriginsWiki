Riven Protoform

The Riven Protoform is a pre-Hardmode enemy that spawns in the Riven Hive. The Protoform follows Fighter AI except occasionally rapidly charging the player and jumping high to reach them.

Trivia

- The Bestiary entry for the Riven Protoform: "The first creature born in the abominable Riven Hive. It is a very agile protector of its home."
