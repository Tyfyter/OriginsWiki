Sinks

If you are looking for the official page of vanilla sinks, click here.

Sinks are functional furniture items and crafting stations that mostly serve as decorations, but can be used to craft some items that require water as a crafting station. The Residue Sink counts as a source of Amebic Gel instead of water despite using a Water Bucket in its recipe.
