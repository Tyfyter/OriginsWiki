Pink Cryosten Armor

Pink Cryosten armor is an armor set consisting of the Pink Cryosten Helmet, Pink Cryosten Breastplate and Pink Cryosten Greaves. Other than major visual alterations, Pink Cryosten armor is identical and interchangeable with regular Cryosten armor. In order to craft the full set the player will need the full Pink Snow armor set, 70 Ice Blocks and 3 Life Crystals.

A full set of Pink Cryosten armor provides the following benefits:

- +12 defense
- +1 HP/s life regeneration
- +40% explosive blast radius
- +8% max max life
- +10% movement speed
- Increased movement speed on ice
Set bonus
- All attacks inflict Frostburn for 5-10 seconds
- +10% explosive velocity
- Immunity to Chilled and Frozen
