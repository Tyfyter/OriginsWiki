Lanterns

If you are looking for the official page of vanilla lanterns, click here.

Lanterns are a type of furniture and light source that emits light when hung from the ceiling. Lanterns take up a 1x2-tile space from the ceiling, making them ideal in smaller areas. Most lanterns can be toggled on and off via wire.
