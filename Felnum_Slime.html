Felnum Slime

The Felnum Slime is a pre-Hardmode enemy that spawns on the Floating Islands in Space after Skeletron has been defeated. The Felnum Slime follows slime AI except with the added ability to perform a lightning strike against the ground, instantly landing and exploding for a decent area. It inflicts Static Shock for 4 seconds on contact with the player and 5 seconds if the player is struck by its lightning attack. Unlike the Felnum Einheri and Guardian, it will not attack other enemies.
