Shattered Goldfish

The Shattered Goldfish is a pre-Hardmode enemy that results from a Goldfish that has been warped, either by the player using Dreadful Powder on it or when a Goldfish spawns during a Blood Moon in a Defiled world. The Shattered Goldfish charges at the player when they're submerged, regenerates life and releases a Defiled Wisp upon death in Master Mode like any other Defiled enemy.
