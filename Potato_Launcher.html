Potato Launcher

[statblock Potato_Launcher]

The Potato Launcher is a pre-Hardmode generic weapon that is sold by the Cubekon Tinkerer NPC at all times. It uses Potatoes as ammunition and simply fires them in an arc. The Potato Battery, Hot Potato and Potato Mine all count as ammunition for the Potato Launcher as well, each with their own unique effects.

Its best modifier is Legendary.
