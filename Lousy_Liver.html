Lousy Liver

The Lousy Liver is a pre-Hardmode accessory that has a 1/87 (1.15%) chance to be dropped by Defiled Cyclopes and Defiled Krushers. While equipped, 4 of the closest enemies to the player are inflicted with Rasterized.
