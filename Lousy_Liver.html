Lousy Liver

[statblock Lousy_Liver]

The Lousy Liver is a pre-Hardmode accessory that has a 1/10 (10%) chance of being dropped by Defiled Ekkoes. While equipped, 4 of the closest enemies to the player are inflicted with Rasterized.
