Ameballoon

The Ameballoon is an explosive that can be crafted at a Work Benches nearby Riven Water with Rubber or can be dropped in smaller amounts from Amebic Slimes and Cleavers, and greater amounts from Mitoworms. They are also sold by the Demolitionist NPC for [price 2|50] after selling him 60 Peat Moss once the World Cracker has been defeated. The Ameballoon explodes into 12-16 droplets that damage enemies.
