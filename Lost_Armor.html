Lost Armor

Lost armor is an armor set consisting of the Lost Monocle, Lost Cloak and Lost Pants. In order to craft the full set the player will need 42 Lost Ore and 66 Strange String.

A full set of Lost armor provides the following benefits:

- +10 defense
- +2 mana regeneration
- +15% magic damage
- +5% movement speed
Set bonus
- +15% mana shielding

Notes

- Lost Armor's effect is stackable with other mana shielding boosts (see item synergies).
- Mana shielding takes into account the damage the player would have received with 0 defense and 0% damage reduction. This results in the proper amount of damage reduced from the attack, but an inefficiency in the mana consumed from it.
- Whenever the player benefits from mana shielding, Defiled Exhaustion will be applied preventing any mana regneration for 3.2 seconds.

Tips

- Lost armor can be paired with Refactoring Pieces to increase its mana shielding by 10%, allowing up to 25% of damage the player receives to be redirected to their mana.
- Lost armor pairs well with Magic Cuffs and reduces the amount of mana consumed from mana shielding by 66%.
