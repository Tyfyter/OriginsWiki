Strange Power-Up

The Strange Power-Up is a developer pet item that has a 1/50 (2%) chance to be dropped by any of the mechanical bosses. It summons a Platformer Mech to accompany the player.

Notes

- The Platformer Mech does not have a flying animation and therefore cannot fly. It will continously teleport to the player when they are separated.

Trivia

- "The very first sprite" tooltip is a reference to chrersis' first sprite ever made. The Platformer Mech is a recreation of that sprite from memory, and is originally a part of her micro-game on Sketch Nation Create called "Steel of War".
- The sound effect for the Platformer Mech spawning is inspired by the power-up collection noise from MechAssault and was made with mouthy noises to recreate the feeling of its source.
