Fae Nymph

The Fae Nymph is a pre-Hardmode enemy that spawns in the Cavern layer of any biome. The Fae Nymph behaves the exact same way as regular Nymphs save for their very jumpy behavior. Fae Nymphs also spawn more commonly near the Aether and appear differently from regular Nymphs as well.

Notes

- Regular Nymphs can be shimmered to and from Fae Nymphs.
