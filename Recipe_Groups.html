Recipe Groups

If you are looking for the official page of vanilla recipe groups, click here.

Recipe groups are a feature added by Origins for specific crafting recipes. Sometimes items featured in a recipe group can be Shimmer cycled into one another. See below for the details of each recipe group.

Any Evil Boomerang

The 'Any Evil Boomerang' recipe group was solely made for the crafting recipe for the Waning Crescent. It includes the Dark Spiral, Hemorang, Krakram, Riverang and Orbital Saw.

Any Evil Boss drop

The 'Any Evil Boss drop' recipe group includes the Shadow Scale, Tissue Sample, Undead Chunk, Riven Carapace and NE-8. Items in this recipe group can be Shimmer cycled.

Any Evil Gun Magazine

The 'Any Evil Gun Magazine' recipe group was solely made for the crafting recipe for the Molten Gun Magazine. It includes the Demonite Gun Magazine, Crimtane Gun Magazine, Defiled Gun Magazine, Encrusted Gun Magazine and Sanguinite Gun Magazine.

Any Evil Herb

The 'Any Evil Herb' recipe group includes Deathweed, the Wilting Rose, Wrycoral and Surveysprout. Items in this recipe group can be Shimmer cycled.

Any Gem Staff

The 'Any Gem Staff' recipe group was solely made for the crafting recipe for the Bled Out Staff, Crimson Rod and Vilethorn. It includes the Amber Staff, Amethyst Staff, Diamond Staff, Emerald Staff, Ruby Staff, Sapphire Staff and Topaz Staff.
