Tolruk

The Tolruk is a Hardmode gun that is crafted using an Uzi and Valkryum Bars. While firing, the Tolruk charges up for a lightning strike every 10 shots. The bolt has infinite pierce and inflicts struck enemies with the Electrified debuff for 3-5 seconds. The Tolruk gets a 50% boost to damage bonuses.

Its best modifier is Unreal.
