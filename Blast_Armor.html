Blast Armor

Blast armor is a Hardmode, post-Mech armor set consisting of the Blast Helmet, Blast Plate and Blast Guards. In order to craft the full set the player will need 54 Chlorophyte Bars, 3 Blast-Resistant Plates, 6 Busted Servos, 4 Rotors and 4 Power Cores.

A full set of Blast armor provides the following benefits:

- +39 defense
- +10% generic critical strike chance
- -15% self-damage
- Immunity to knockback
- +25% movement speed
- +2% acceleration/deceleration
- +20% jump speed
Set bonus
- -25% self-damage
- Allows the player to berserk by pressing the ability key
- 80% of incoming damage and self-damage increases the berserk duration
- All explosive combat stats are increased by 50% for berserk duration
- The helmet changes its appearance while berserked
