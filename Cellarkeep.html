Cellarkeep

The Cellarkeep is an enemy found in the Dungeon. They behave and look similar to their Angry Bones counterparts, except that they will throw rolling explosive barrels whenever a player is within range. The explosive barrels they throw explode when making contact with the player or a high-enough wall, dealing splash damage and destroying tiles for a considerable area.

Tips

- Unlike the strategies used to avoid Angry Bones, Cellarkeeps are capable of throwing their barrels upwards to hit the player. Because of this, it is advisable to keep your distance.
