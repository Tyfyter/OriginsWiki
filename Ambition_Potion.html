Ambition Potion

The Ambition Potion is a buff potion that can be crafted using Bottled Water, a Polyeel and any evil herb. When consumed, it provides the Ambition buff, which increases damage, critical strike chances, and weapon speeds by 2.5% whilst also providing 12.5% stat share. This effect will last for 4 minutes, but can be canceled anytime by right-clicking the icon.
