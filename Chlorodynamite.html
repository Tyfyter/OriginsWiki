Chlorodynamite

Chlorodynamite is a post-Golem explosive that can be crafted using Chlorophyte Ore or Chlorophyte Bars and Dynamite. Before detonation, vines will reach out from the projectile and attempt to pull in any nearby enemies towards it. Chlorodynamite then explodes into a flash of light that inflicts Confused for 3.67 seconds and Slow for 5 seconds, similarly to the Flashbang.
