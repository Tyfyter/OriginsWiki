Amebolize Incantation

The Amebolize Incantation is a pre-Hardmode incantation that has a 1/6 (16.67%) chance to be dropped by destroyed Gel Blisters in the Riven Hive. The incantation will summon an amoeba bubble to wherever the mouse is when the Use/Attack button is pressed and held. The amoeba bubble can be dumbfired by simply pressing the same button. The amoeba bubble will magnetize to enemies and pierce 5 times.

The best modifier is Mythical.
