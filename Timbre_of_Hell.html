Timbre of Hell

Timbre of Hell is a pre-Hardmode accessory that has a 1/48 (2.08%) chance of being dropped by a Demon. While equipped and when the player is afflicted with the On Fire! debuff, all damage from player-inflicted debuffs is multiplied by 3 besides the On Fire! debuff, which instead has its damage multiplied by 6.

Timbre of Hell is a lore item indicated by "You hear whispers nearby..."
