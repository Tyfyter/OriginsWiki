Brine Cheese

Brine Cheese is a food item that has a 1/8 (12.5%) chance to drop from any enemies in the Brine Pool during April Fools. When consumed, it provides the Exquisitely Stuffed buff, which lasts for 12 minutes. Additionally, it will inflict Feral Bite for 12 minutes and 2 seconds, and Tipsy for 12 minutes.
