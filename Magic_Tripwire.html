Magic Tripwire

The Magic Tripwire is a pre-Hardmode accessory found in Underground Deadman's Chests. It is also sold by the Demolitionist NPC for [price 10|0|0] after selling him 135 Peat Moss. It causes explosive projectiles to detonate when enemies are within their blast radius.
