Ancient Defiled Phantom

The Ancient Defiled Phantom is a pre-Hardmode enemy that rarely spawns in the Defiled Wastelands. The Ancient Defiled Phantom takes on the appearance of one of the older iterations of the Defiled Phantom, with a notably larger appearance. It lacks life regen, the noises Defiled enemies typically make and has a more basic method of movement than its modern iteration. Ancient Defiled Phantoms are a good source for Ancient Defiled armor as the armor set is 38 times more likely to be dropped from it than other Defiled enemies.
