Alkaline Canister

The Alkaline Canister is a type of Hardmode canister used as ammunition for launchers and the Proximity Mine Launcher. Upon landing on a tile or enemy, the Alkaline Canister will explode into brine droplets that each have a small chance to inflict Toxic Shock.
