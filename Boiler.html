Boiler

The Boiler is a pre-Hardmode ranged weapon that can be found in Shadow Chests or Obsidian Lock Boxes. It fires a 2-round burst of lava that inflicts Oiled for 0.5-1 second and On Fire! for 5-10 seconds. It uses Fireblossoms as ammo.

Its best modifier is Unreal or Heavy Caliber.
