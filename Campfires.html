Campfires

If you are looking for the official page of vanilla campfires, click here.

[statblock Campfires]

Campfires are functional furniture items that emit light and provide the Cozy Fire health regeneration buff to players nearby them. A Campfire can be turned off by pressing the Open/Activate on the object or by sending it a wire signal, and can be toggled back on again the same way.
