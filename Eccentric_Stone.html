Eccentric Stone

The Eccentric Stone is a pre-Hardmode accessory given to the player by the Zoologist NPC after completing her 'Under the Starlight' quest. Equipping it does nothing, however, it will react and turn into a Spirit Shard when the player is nearby a Dark Lamia.

The Eccentric Stone is a lore item indicated by "You hear whispers nearby..."
