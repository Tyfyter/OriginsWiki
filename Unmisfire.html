Unmisfire

The Unmisfire is a pre-Hardmode bow that can be crafted using Defiled Bars and Undead Chunks. It is the Defiled-equivalent to the Corruption's Demon Bow, the Crimson's Tendon Bow and the Riven's Threemoepeater. It behaves like an ordinary bow, but near misses from this weapon will teleport back to the enemy and attempt to strike it again to rectify the missed shot. If an arrow that has piercing properties is used, it will allow that arrow to strike the enemy twice.

Its best modifier is Deadly or Heavy Caliber.
