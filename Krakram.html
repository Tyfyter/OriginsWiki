Krakram

The Krakram is a pre-Hardmode boomerang that is crafted with Defiled Bars, or dropped by Defiled Relays with a 1/5 (20%) chance. It also has a 1/5 (20%) chance of being obtained from Chunky and Bilious Crates fished in the Defiled Wastelands.

Its best modifier is Godly or Demonic.
