Refactoring Pieces

Refactoring Pieces is a pre-Hardmode accessory that is obtained from the Treasure Bag dropped by the Defiled Amalgamation in Expert Mode. While this is equipped, the player will have 10% of damage they recieve redirected to mana. Additionally, if Lost armor is not equipped, the player will also recieve a 5% increase in damage reduction.

Notes

- Refactoring Pieces' effect is stackable with other mana shielding boosts (see item synergies).
- Mana shielding takes into account the damage the player would have received with 0 defense and 0% damage reduction. This results in the proper amount of damage reduced from the attack, but an inefficiency in the mana consumed from it.
- Whenever the player benefits from mana shielding, Defiled Exhaustion will be applied preventing any mana regneration for 3.2 seconds.
