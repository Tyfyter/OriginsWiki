Bolt Gun

The Tiny Sniper is a Hardmode gun that has a 4.17% chance to be dropped by Skeleton Snipers. It behaves similarly to the Sniper Rifle except it knocks the player back when fired.

Its best modifier is Unreal.

Tips

With enough attack speed boosts, the knockback from the Tiny Sniper speeds you up faster than you lose momentum. This allows for extremely high speeds to be reached.
