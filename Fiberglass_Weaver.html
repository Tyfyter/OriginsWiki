Fiberglass Weaver

The Fiberglass Weaver is a pre-Hardmode, Fiberglass-themed boss. It is summoned when a player uses Shaped Glass anywhere in the Fiberglass Undergrowth.

The Fiberglass Weaver has several different attacks it uses during its boss fight. When not attacking, it will slowly charge at the player instead. Its behaviors can be explained further in the section below.

Defeating the Fiberglass Weaver drops different pieces of Fiberglass Armor and other fiberglass equipment.

Behavior

The Fiberglass Weaver will perform many manuevers to get the jump on the player or put them in awkward situations using its thread. Movement speed is disencouraged when fighting the Weaver over calculated planning and strategy. Below are some of its attacks that it can perform.

Attacks

  • Lunge - The Fiberglass Weaver will charge up for a lunge at the player's current position if they get too far from it.
  • Thread Trap - The Fiberglass Weaver will spin up a thread trap that it uses to trap the player into a confined space. This attack can be telegraphed when the Weaver uses its front two legs in a spinning motion before finishing the attack. The threads deal high damage and knockback the player, leaving the only option to just avoid them outright.

Special

The Fiberglass Weaver has a chance inflict the Webbed debuff on the player for 0.17 seconds.

- The chance of inflicting Webbed is increased from 6.25% to 12.5%.
- The duration of Webbed applied to struck players is increased from 0.17s to 0.34s.

- The Weaver's lunge speed is increased by 34%.
- The Weaver uses its thread trap 40% more.

Notes

- The Fiberglass Weaver will pursue the player even outside of the Undergrowth.
