Barnacleback

The Barnacleback is a pre-Hardmode enemy that spawns in the Riven Hive. It will hover above the ground and charge at the player while increasing the speed, jump height, defense and life regeneration of all nearby Riven enemies. Below are the specific details of its boost:

- Movement speed and jump height: +20%
- Defense: +5
- Life regeneration: +6 HP/s
