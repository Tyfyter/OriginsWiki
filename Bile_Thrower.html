Bile Thrower

The Bile Thrower is an enemy derived from a Penguin that has been corrupted, either by the player using Dreadful Powder or by a Blood Moon event on a Defiled world. The Bile Thrower behaves similarly to its vanilla counterparts: the Corrupt Penguin and Vicious Penguin, except that it can now spit bile-like projectiles at the player to inflict Slow for 2 seconds.

Notes

- Although they spawn during the Blood Moon event, they cannot drop Bloody Tears.
