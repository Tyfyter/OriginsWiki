Helium Tank

The Helium Tank is a Hardmode accessory combining a Whoopie Cushion and an Air Tank. While worn, it provides the player with immunity to Suffocation and an additional 25 seconds of breath. It does nothing different from its original unless the player takes damage in which it will make the player character's voice a higher pitch.
