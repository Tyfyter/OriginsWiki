Asylum Whistle

The Asylum Whistle is a pre-Hardmode accessory found in Locked Gold Chests stowed away in the Dungeon. It allows the player to tag two different enemies at once whilst increasing minion damage by 10%.

The Asylum Whistle is a lore item indicated by "You hear whispers nearby..."
