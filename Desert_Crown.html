Desert Crown

The Desert Crown is a pre-Hardmode summon weapon that can be found in the chest of a Pyramid. It summons a sand elemental minion which attacks by approaching enemies and casting a cloud of sand at them. The Desert Crown might be preferred over its equivalents for the high knockback the sand elemental's attack applies.

Desert Crown's best modifier is Mythical. The Sand Elemental deals moderate damage that can benefit from damage bonuses, but its best attribute is its knockback potential.

Lore

- The Desert Crown is comprised of hardened sand with emeralds cast inside of the structure. It was likely used as a status symbol by some powerful entity within the pyramid's civilization.
