Sea Dragon

The Sea Dragon is a Hardmode enemy that spawns in the Brine Pool. The Sea Dragon will quickly swim towards the player and attack them. When the player leaves water while being chased after, it will jump out of water to reach the player. Getting hit by a Sea Dragon inflicts Acid Venom for 2-3 seconds and Toxic Shock for 3-4.

Sea Dragons prey upon Brine Latchers and are preyed upon themselves by Carpalfish, Brine Serpents and Mildew Creepers. When met with a predator (besides the player), they will attempt to flee.
