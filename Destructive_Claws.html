Destructive Claws

Destructive Claws are a pre-Hardmode accessory crafted using Feral Claws and the Bomb Handling Device. It increases the velocity of thrown explosives by 25% and their attack speed by 10%. Additionally, it enables auto-use for all explosive weapons.
