Traffic Cone

The Traffic Cone is a small furniture item that has a 1/10 (10%) chance to be dropped by Conehead Zombies. It can also be crafted using Rubber at a Heavy Work Bench. When placed, any players or enemies within a 30-tile radius of the cone will be constantly inflicted with the Slow debuff.
