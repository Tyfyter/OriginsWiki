Trijaw Shark

The Trijaw Shark is a Hardmode enemy that spawns in the Eutrophic Sea or Deserts overrun by the Riven during a Sandstorm. The Trijaw Shark behaves similarly to other Sand Sharks just also acting as an equivalent to Sharks in the Eutrophic Sea.

Trivia

- The Bestiary entry for the Trijaw Shark: "This menacing shark has adapted to the Hive's ecosystem quite well, taking on a triple-mandible design used for crushing and cracking nautili caught in its path."
- The Trijaw Shark's current sprite was a recolor and restoration of its old sprite when it was known as the "Riven Splitter". The old sprite was taken from when the Riven Hive was first introduced.
