Bee Afraid Incantation

[statblock Bee_Afraid_Incantation]

The Bee Afraid Incantation is a pre-Hardmode incantation that has a 1/4 (25%) chance to be dropped by Queen Bee, sharing its loot pool with her other typical drops. The incantation will summon a swarm of frenzying bees to wherever the mouse is when the Use/Attack button is pressed and held. Like most other incantations, however, the swarm can be dumbfired by simply pressing the same button. The swarm will rapidly deal damage and inflict minor amounts of knockback.

The best modifier is Mythical.
