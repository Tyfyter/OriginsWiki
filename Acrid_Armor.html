Acrid Armor

Acrid armor is a Hardmode armor set. It has melee, ranged, magic, summoner and explosive set versions, depending on the equipped headpiece. In order to craft the full individual set the player will need 59 Eitrite Bars and 60 Rubber, however, the player will need 111 Eitrite Bars and 100 Rubber total in order to craft all pieces of the set.

A full set of Acrid armor provides the following benefits:

Acrid Dome
- +34 defense
- +10% explosive critical strike chance (+8% more while submerged)
- +10% explosive velocity (+8% more while submerged)
- Increased visibility while submerged
- Emit an aura of light
- +2 HP/s life regeneration
- Grants the ability to swim

Acrid Headgear
- +36 defense
- +10% magic damage (+8% more while submerged)
- -10% mana cost (-10% more while submerged)
- Increased visibility while submerged
- Emit an aura of light
- +2 HP/s life regeneration
- Grants the ability to swim

Acrid Helm
- +50 defense
- +10% melee damage (+8% more while submerged)
- +10% melee attack speed (+8% more while submerged)
- Increased visibility while submerged
- Emit an aura of light
- +2 HP/s life regeneration
- Grants the ability to swim

Acrid Mask
- +33 defense
- +10% summon damage (+8% more while submerged)
- +1 minion slot (+1 more while submerged)
- Increased visibility while submerged
- Emit an aura of light
- +2 HP/s life regeneration
- Grants the ability to swim

Acrid Visor
- +41 defense
- +20% ranged attack speed (+16% more while submerged)
- Increased visibility while submerged
- Emit an aura of light
- +2 HP/s life regeneration
- Grants the ability to swim

Set bonus
- All attacks inflict Toxic Shock
- Immunity to Acid Venom, Poisoned and Toxic Shock
- +6 seconds of breath
