Crystal Cutters

The Crystal Cutters is a Hardmode melee weapon crafted using crystal shards, magic hair spray, and rubber at an anvil. When used, it will fire a fast, crystalline projectile with each swing. The projectile has a lifespan of 2 seconds and will bounce off walls and peirce through 6 targets. It is used in the "To Be Crystal Clear..." quest. The best modifier is Legendary.
