Crystal Cutters

The Crystal Cutters is a Hardmode melee weapon crafted using crystal shards, Magic Hair Spray and Rubber at an Anvil. When used it will fire a fast crystalline orb with each swing. The projectile has a lifespan of 2 seconds and will bounce off walls, piercing 6 times. It is used in the "To Be Crystal Clear..." quest.

Its best modifier is Legendary.
