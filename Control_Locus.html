Control Locus

The Control Locus is a pre-Hardmode accessory combining the Stack of Shraps with the Weakpoint Analyzer. While equipped, all ranged projectiles are duplicated 5 times while explosive projectiles are duplicated twice. All of the duplicated projectiles have a chance to deal damage based on the player's ammo conservation chance of which the Control Locus provides an additional 12% chance. Weapons qualifying as ranged-explosive benefit from both duplications.
