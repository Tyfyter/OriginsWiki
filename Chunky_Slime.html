Chunky Slime

The Chunky Slime is a pre-Hardmode enemy that spawns in the Defiled Wastelands. The Chunky Slime behaves like other slime except with regeneration and the release of a Defiled Wisp in Master Mode.

Trivia

- The Bestiary entry for the Chunky Slime: "A slime desaturated of its color due to contact with black bile. It now spends the rest of its days to the Defiled will."
