The Bird

The Bird is a Hardmode developer, melee weapon that has a 1/20 (5%) chance to be obtained from Treasure Bags dropped from Hardmode bosses (except Queen Slime's). It has a time attack based on a specific rhythm of use and a launch attack that punts enemies into the air. Whenever the Attack button is released as the star indicator appears on The Bird's sprite, its damage will be greatly increased for that swing. Swinging while perfoming a time attack before and after the indicator result in no damage increase.

Additionally, enemies struck by this weapon receive great amounts of knockback and damage any enemies that are in their trajectory after getting struck. Essentially, enemies struck by this weapon can collide with other enemies with both receiving damage in the process.

Its best modifier is Legendary.
