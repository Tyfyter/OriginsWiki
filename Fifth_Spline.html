Fifth Spline

The Fifth Spline is a Hardmode gun that can be crafted using Adamantite Bars or Titanium Bars with Respyrite and Scrap. It quickly fires random projectiles with different effects depending on what is fired. Lug nuts have no effect, wrenches Slow enemies, piston arms have increased knockback, soldering irons inflict both Bleeding and On Fire! for 5 seconds, and batteries inflict Static Shock for 5 seconds. All projectiles fired by this weapon are affected by gravity.

Its best modifier is Unreal or Heavy Caliber.
