Playtime's Over

Playtime's Over is a Hardmode accessory combining a Bug Trapper and Fleshy Figurine. When equipped, the player's life regeneration, defense and inflicted Torn severity will be increased depending on the current severity of Torn being applied to them. On its own, however, it grants the ability for all of the player's attacks to inflict Torn while increasing the severity of inflicted Torn by 20%. The highest amount of severity the player can inflict with only Playtime's Over receiving 99% Torn severity is 35.82%. This effect is stackable with other boosts in Torn severity.
