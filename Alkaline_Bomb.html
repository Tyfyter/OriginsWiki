Alkaline Bomb

The Alkaline Bomb is a type of Hardmode explosive that can be crafted using Bombs and Bottled Brine or purchased from the Demolitionist NPC after selling 230 Peat Moss to him once Hardmode is reached. The alkaline bomb explodes into 3-6 brine droplets that each inflict Toxic Shock lasting anywhere from 4.5 to 6 seconds.
