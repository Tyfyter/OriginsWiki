Riven Pigron

The Riven Pigron is a Hardmode enemy that spawns in an Ice biome when it is overlapped with the Underground Riven Hive. It has the same behavior as other Pigrons.

Trivia

- The Bestiary entry for the Riven Pigron: "This elusive dragon-pig hybrid has excellent stealth capabilities despite its rotund figure. It is uncertain how they came to exist."
