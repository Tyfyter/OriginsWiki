Impactaxe

The Impactaxe is a post-mech pickaxe that is crafted using Busted Servoes, a Power Core, Rotors and Rubber at a Fabricator. Like all pickaxes, it is solely used to destroy tiles. When it comes in contact with a surface, it will detonate the tiles around it for a decent area and deal self-damage to the user.
