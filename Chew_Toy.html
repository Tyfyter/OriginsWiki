Chew Toy

The Chew Toy is a developer item pet item that has a 1/20 (5%) chance to be obtained from Treasure Bags dropped from Hardmode bosses (except Queen Slime's). It summons a Chee Toy to accompany the player, which appears as a worm-like alien creature. The Chee Toy also speaks to the player within certain contexts.
