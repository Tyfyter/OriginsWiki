Living Fire Blocks

If you are looking for the official page of vanilla Living Fire Blocks, click here.

Living Fire Blocks are animated background furniture blocks that, when placed, resemble small animated flames and emit light. They deal no damage on contact and can be freed with any pickaxe or drill.
