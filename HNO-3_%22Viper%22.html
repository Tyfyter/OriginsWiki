HNO-3 "Viper"

The HNO-3 "Viper" is a Hardmode gun that can be crafted using Eitrite Bars. It fires glowing, green bullets that inflict Toxic Shock for 7.5 seconds. When an enemy is struck by this weapon while affected by a debuff other than Toxic Shock, they are guaranteed to receive a critical hit.

Its best modifier is Unreal or Heavy Caliber.
