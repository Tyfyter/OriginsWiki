Weakpoint Analyzer

The Weakpoint Analyzer is a pre-Hardmode accessory that is dropped by the Brain of Cthulhu in Master Mode. While equipped, all ranged projectiles wil be duplicated 2 times and approach their targets from random directions. Each of the 3 duplicated projectiles will have an equal chance to that of the player's ammo conservation rate to actually damage the enemy. Additionally, the Weakpoint Analyzer boosts the player's ammo conservation rate by 8%.

Tips

- The Weakpoint Analyzer works well with harpoon guns, duplicating their projectiles but not consuming any ammo when one of the duplicated harpoons are destroyed. The duplicated projectiles still behave like normal projectiles with the exception of a few custom projectiles like that of the Vipershot. The best synergy is with the Boat Rocker.
