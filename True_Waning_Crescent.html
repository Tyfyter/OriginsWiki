True Waning Crescent

The True Waning Crescent is a Hardmode boomerang that is crafted with Souls of Fright, Souls of Might, Souls of Sight and the Waning Crescent. When thrown it will leave shadowy after-images of itself as it approaches its maximum range that can infinitely pierce enemies and damage them upon contact. Whenever enemies draw near the after-images, they will fire glowing, green copies of themselves at that enemy.

Its best modifier is Godly or Demonic.
