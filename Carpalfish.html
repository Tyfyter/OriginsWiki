Carpalfish

The Carpalfish is a Hardmode enemy that spawns in the Brine Pool. It will swim like most fish and charge at the player at a fast speed after building-up the attack. If the Carpalfish collides with a solid surface while attempting to hit the player, it has a chance to drop a Venom Fang. This can be indicated by a loud thud noise.

Carpalfish prey upon Sea Dragons and Leaf Snappers. Depsite being prey to Brine Serpents, Mildew Creepers and King Crabs they will fight back instead of fleeing like most Brine Pool creatures.
