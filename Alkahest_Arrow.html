Alkahest Arrow

The Alkahest Arrow is a type of Hardmode arrow fired by bows or repeaters. The arrows carry their namesake Alkahest component inside of a glass tip, shattering on impact to release alkahest on the target. Alkahest arrows inflict Torn with a 50% severity on impact.
