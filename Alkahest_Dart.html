Alkahest Dart

The Alkahest Dart is a type of Hardmode dart fired by dart launchers. Alkahest darts release clouds of alkahest that remain in place for a short moment and inflict Torn to enemies with a severity of 35%. Enemies directly struck by the dart will instead be inflicted with a Torn of 60%.
