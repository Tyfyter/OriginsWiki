Torn

Torn is a debuff that is applied by most items and enemies found in the Riven Hive. When applied to an NPC, they will receive more damage from the player depending on the severity of the Torn being applied. When applied to a player, they will instead have their max life according to the severity applied. When the effects of Torn have elapsed, the player will have to regain all of the health lost from Torn through their own means.

Notes

- When afflicted by different variants of Torn, the highest severity and longest duration will override any previous amounts applied to an NPC or player.
