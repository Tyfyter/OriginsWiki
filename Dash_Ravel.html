Dash Ravel

Dash Ravel is a Hardmode accessory that is crafted using a Tabi and a Ravel. Dash Ravel turns the player into a small sphere, upon double-tapping the Down button, that provides slightly more speed than their normal form and gives a 10% chance to dodge attacks. Dash Ravel allows the player to dash anytime while this is equipped, but with a small delay whenever the player is not already ravelled. Dash Ravel deals damage and knocks back enemies whenever it reaches top speed, which can be quickly reached by dashing.
