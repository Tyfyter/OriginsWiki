Defiled Asphyxiator

The Defiled Asphyxiator is a Hardmode enemy that occasionally spawns in an Ocean overtaken by the Defiled Wastelands. The Defiled Asphyxiator will summon and hurdle boulders of Defiled Stone at the player that inflict cumulative effects as the player gets hit. The boulders will inflict various slowing debuffs including Rasterized until the player gets hit a third time, where the player will lose all of their breath. The Asphyxiator will maintain a certain distance from the player at all times, except for when the player is force-drowned where it will charge and attempt to eat the player.
