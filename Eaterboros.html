Eaterboros

The Eaterboros is a Pre-Hardmode melee weapon that has a 1/2 (50%) chance to be dropped by the Eater of Worlds. While using the item, it will start with 3 segments and gain a segments up to a max of 8 segments, spending mana each time. When you release the item or hit the cap, it will swing with all segments past the one that hit the enemy. The detached segments will home in on enemies and deal additional damage.

Its best modifiers are Legendary and Mythical. Legendary increases the range by 1 tile at maximum size, while Mythical makes the weapon use 1 less mana per segment grown.
