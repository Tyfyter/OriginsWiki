Mojo Injection

Mojo Injection is a permanent booster that is vital for handling Assimilation inflicted onto the player by the world evils. It is obtained by shimmering a Nature's Gift, and can only be used once per player. When used, the player's assimilation will deplete at a rate of 0.2664% each second, meaning it takes 6 minutes and 12 seconds to fully recover from being 99% assimilated. Like all sources of assimilation management, all types of assimilation are affected by the full amount of healing rather than being divided amongst each other.
