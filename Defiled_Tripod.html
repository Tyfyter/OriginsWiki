Defiled Tripod

The Defiled Tripod is a Hardmode enemy that spawns in the Defiled Wastelands. The Tripod is a fast and dangerous Defiled enemy that inflicts Rasterized for 1 second upon contact with the player, making it hard to escape. Additionally, Defiled Tripods are capable of going through all tiles and climbing walls to reach the player.

Tips

- The best way to handle a Defiled Tripod is by killing it quickly.
- Alternatively, the player can equip Tripod Nip to make them friendly.

Trivia


- The Defiled Tripod was originally planned to be a slow, projectile-shooting enemy, but it was implemented to be as it is now because of how it was sprited.
