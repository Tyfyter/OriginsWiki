Defiled Cyclops

The Defiled Cyclops is a pre-Hardmode enemy that spawns in the Defiled Wastelands. The Defiled Cyclops slowly approaches the player, regenerates life and releases a Defiled Wisp upon death in Master Mode like any other Defiled enemy.

Notes

- Even though it appears to be 4 tiles tall, it can fit through 3-tile spaces similarly to the Face Monster.
