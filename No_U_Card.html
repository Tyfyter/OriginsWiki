No U Card

The No U Card is a Hardmode immunity accessory crafted using an Ankh Charm and a Return To Sender. It retaliates against enemies that have struck the player, returning half of the damage recieved and applying any debuffs that would've been applied to the player. Additionally, it grants the player immunity to the following debuffs:
