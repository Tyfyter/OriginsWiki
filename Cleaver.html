Cleaver

The Cleaver is a pre-Hardmode enemy that spawns in the Riven Hive. The Cleaver follows the Worm AI, and benefits from a slight speed boost. It spawns in groups of 2-3 from slain Mitoworms.

Trivia

- The Bestiary entry for the Cleaver: "A vicious defender of the Riven Hive, the Cleaver hides in burrows of spug flesh awaiting any trespassers of its territory."
