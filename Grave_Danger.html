Grave Danger

[statblock Grave_Danger]

The Grave Danger is a Hardmode shield accessory that has a 1/40 (2.5%) chance of being dropped by any enemies that spawn in the Graveyard biome. It provides 5 flat defense at all times and a 25% boost to the player's defense after death. On Hardcore difficulty, Grave Danger instead provides the player with 15% damage reduction on top of the flat defense increase.

Notes

- Using a Candlelight while Grave Danger is equipped causes a unique entity to spawn that will follow the player.
