Impact Bomb

The Impact Bomb is an explosive that can be crafted using Bombs and Peat Moss or purchased from the Demolitionist NPC after selling 70 Peat Moss to him. The Impact Bomb has an increased velocity from regular bombs and detonates after colliding with a tile or enemy, or after 3 seconds.
