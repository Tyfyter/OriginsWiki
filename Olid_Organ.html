Olid Organ

The Olid Organ is a Hardmode accessory combining a Putrid Scent with a Decaying Scale. While equipped, the player has 5% increased damage and critical strike chances and have decreased aggro from enemies. Additionally, all attacks from the player inflict Toxic Shock.
