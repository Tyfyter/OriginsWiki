Single-Cellular Nautilus

The Single-Cellular Nautilus is a pre-Hardmode enemy that spawns in the Riven Hive. The Nautilus rolls around and charges at the player while benefitting from the increased defense of its shell.

Trivia

- The Bestiary entry for the Single-Cellular Nautilus: "This Riven-infested nautilus has grown accustomed to the amebic water's viscosity making it very agile on land. The sharpness of its shell is an added danger."
