Bombardment

The Bombardment is a pre-Hardmode launcher that can be crafted using Defiled Bars and Undead Chunks. The Bombardment converts its ammunition into hovering explosives, that remain in place for 7 seconds before exploding or until coming to contact with an enemy. It also has a 50% chance not to consume ammo.

Its best modifier is Guileless.
