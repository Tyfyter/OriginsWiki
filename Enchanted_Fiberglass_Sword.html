Enchanted Fiberglass Sword

The Enchanted Fiberglass Sword is a pre-Hardmode enemy that spawns in the Fiberglass Undergrowth. The Sword will fly around in search for the player where it will prepare for a strike and spin at the player. After striking the player, it will spin out of control and get stuck into a nearby surface for a couple of seconds before lodging itself out to attack again.
