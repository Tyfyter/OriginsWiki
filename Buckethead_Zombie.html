Buckethead Zombie

The Buckethead Zombie is a pre-Hardmode enemy that spawns on the surface at night. It is better protected against damage due to the bucket on its head. It has no other behavior from other Zombies.

Trivia

- The Bestiary entry for the Buckethead Zombie: "Buckethead zombie always wore a bucket. Part of it was to assert his uniqueness in an uncaring world. Mostly he just forgot it was there in the first place."
- As hinted at by its bestiary entry, the Buckethead Zombie is inspired by an enemy of the same likeness in Plants vs Zombies.
