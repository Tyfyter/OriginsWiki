Focus Crystal

The Focus Crystal is a Hardmode, Expert Mode-exclusive, post-Golem accessory crafted using a Shiny Stone and the Ruby Reticle. While equipped, the player's life regeneration is boosted depending on damage the player deals, their critical strike chance is increased by 15% of the current weapon's base damage, and weapon damage is increased while the player stands still.
