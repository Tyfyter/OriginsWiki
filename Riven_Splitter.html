Riven Splitter

The Riven Splitter is a pre-Hardmode harpoon gun. It is one of the possible drops from destroying a Riven Lesion or Gel Blister. It is normally obtained alongside 99 Harpoons to be used from the harpoon gun.

Its best modifier is Godly or Demonic.
