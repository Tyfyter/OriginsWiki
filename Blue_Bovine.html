Blue Bovine

Blue Bovine is a food item that is sold by the Merchant NPC after completing his 'To the Skies!' quest. When consumed, it provides the Bovine Flight buff, which gives the player a temporary set of wings. This effect will last for 4 minutes, but can be canceled anytime by right-clicking the icon.

Like most wings, the soda wings allow the player to slow their fall by holding down the Jump key and will not take any fall damage. Its flight speed and power are overall stronger than Fledgling Wings, but it is rather expensive to obtain Blue Bovine as a drawback.

Trivia

- Blue Bovine is a reference to the Red Bull energy drink and its slogan "Red Bull Gives You Wings".
