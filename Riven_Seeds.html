Riven Seeds

Riven Seeds are seeds which can be planted on Dirt Blocks or Mud Blocks to create Riven Grass and Riven Jungle Grass respectively, which will then spread the Riven in all directions. They drop from the Eye of Cthulhu and can be purchased from the Dryad NPC for 500 each during a Blood Moon in a world with the Riven. In a world with any other world evil, Riven Seeds can be purchased from the Dryad if she is spoken to in a Hardmode Graveyard.
