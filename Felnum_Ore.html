Felnum Ore

Felnum Ore is a type of pre-Hardmode ore block found in Floating Islands once Skeletron has been defeated. Felnum Ore requires at least 65% pickaxe power to mine.

Felnum Ore generates electrical sparks when placed that light up the area.
