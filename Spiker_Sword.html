Spiker Sword

The Spiker Sword is a pre-Hardmode melee weapon that is crafted using Defiled Bars. Striking an the same enemy 3 times in a 2-second period causes spikes to grow out of the enemy, dealing great damage.

Its best modifier is Legendary.
