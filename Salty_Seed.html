Salty Seed

The Salty Seed is a Hardmode utility that can be obtained by shimmering the Plasma Cutter into it. It takes up the player's hook slot and is used by pressing the Grapple button. When used, the player will throw a node seed that is quickly enveloped in amebic gel and hovers in place. Any projectiles (and minions by extension) that pass through it are temporarily cloned and both projectiles are rotated by 15 degrees outward. Cloned projectiles will remain present for 5 seconds with the node itself lasting 15 seconds before needing to be redeployed again at no cost.
