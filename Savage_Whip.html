Savage Whip

The Savage Whip is a Hardmode enemy that spawns in the Underground Riven Hive. It behaves similarly to the Enchanted Sword, but will spin around like a whip whenever it gets close enough to the player.

Trivia

- The Bestiary entry for the Savage Whip: "The Savage Whip cursed by the Primordial Amoeba, now serves to protect the caverns of the Hive from intruders."
