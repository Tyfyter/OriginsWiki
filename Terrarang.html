Terrarang

The Terrarang is a Hardmode boomerang that is crafted with a Broken Hero Sword, the True Light Disc and the True Waning Crescent. It inherits the effects of both the True Light Disc and Waning Crescent, but with visual changes to appear more brightly-colored.

Its best modifier is Godly or Demonic.
