Petrified Tree

Petrified Trees are a variant of trees grown on Defiled Grass and Defiled Jungle Grass blocks found at the Defiled Wastelands. Petrified Trees drop Endowood as a dedicated wood item upon destruction and are chopped down using axes or chainsaws. Additionally, Defiled Trees have a 143/2000 (7.015%) chance to drop a Bileberry or Prickly Pear in normal conditions. During the Halloween event, these chances are slightly reduced to a 34073/500000 (6.8146%). Petrified Prickly Pears also have a 3/100 (3%) chance to drop from Petrified Tress on top of all of that. Petrified Trees can be planted anew by planting Acorns on Defiled Grass.
