Scrap Barrier

The Scrap Barrier is a Hardmode shield accessory that is currently unobtainable.

The cursed version provides a 50% damage reduction while above 90% of total health. It also applies a 3-second buffer before life regeneration occurs (even from other accessories) after taking damage while above 50% of total health.

The normal version instead applies a 15% damage reduction and a life regeneration boost.
