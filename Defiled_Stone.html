Defiled Stone

Defiled Stone is a block type found in the Defiled Wastelands. It is the Defiled-equivalent to Stone, and can be converted to other variants with any thrown powder. Before Hardmode, it is most prominent on the surface being the main material of the massive spike-like protrusions. It is also the primary material for the veins underground. Defiled Stone requires at least 65% pickaxe power or explosives to mine.

Notes

- Defiled Stone can be purified, but will quickly reclaim lost ground. This is a part of the Defiled Resurgence mechanic where the Defiled cannot be cleansed until all Defiled Hearts are removed from the world.

Trivia

- For immersion reasons, the sound Defiled Stone makes when being mined and destroyed is the same sound used by Defiled creatures when hurt.
