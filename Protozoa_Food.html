Protozoa Food

Protozoa Food is a pre-Hardmode accessory that is obtained from the Treasure Bag dropped by the World Cracker in Expert Mode. While equipped, the player will generate amoeba pellets to fight for them based on how many minion slots they have without actually taking up any slots. The pellets will kamikaze into enemies and inflict Slow, and will replenish over time. Additionally, the player will gain one extra minion slot.
