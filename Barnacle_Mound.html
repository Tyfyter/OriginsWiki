Barnacle Mound

The Barnacle Mound is a Hardmode enemy that spawns in the Riven Hive. The Mound remains in place from where it spawned and spawns in a group of 4-7 Amoeba Buggers every 10/9/7 seconds. It will not stop producing Amoeba Buggers until it is destroyed. It releases Amoeba Buggies for the player to catch, providing an infinite source of them as long as Barnacle Mounds can spawn. Barnacle Mounds are furthermore the only source for Amoeba Buggers to spawn naturally in a world, and cannot be used to farm for Coins as Amoeba Buggers drop nothing.
