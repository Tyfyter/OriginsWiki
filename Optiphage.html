Optiphage

The Optiphage is a pre-Hardmode enemy that spawns in the Corruption. The Optiphage will fly towards the player's eye and latch onto it when close. While latched to the player's eye the player will quickly accrue Corrupt Assimilation and damage.

Trivia

- The Bestiary entry for the Optiphage: "This Eater is in its first stage of the Eater life cycle. It goes straight for the eyes of its victims in hopes that it can prompt its own eye to grow in."
