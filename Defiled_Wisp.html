Defiled Wisp

The Defiled Wisp is a pre-Hardmode enemy that spawns from downed Defiled enemies in Master Mode, or in a group of 4-5 after slaying the Defiled Amalgamation. They will charge the player until they are destroyed.

Trivia

- The Bestiary entry for the Defiled Wisp: "A rare entity that is released only once its former vessel has been dislodged from its bile interior."
