Resin Shield

The Resin Shield is a Hardmode shield accessory created with Amber, the Obsidian Shield and Carburite. It provides immunity to knockback and the Burning debuff while also periodically blocking all self-damage on a 3.3 second cooldown. The cooldown triggers anytime the player would have recieved an instance of self-damage. The Resin Shield loses its color whenever it is on cooldown.
