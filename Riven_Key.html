Riven Key

The Riven Key is a Hardmode consumable item that has a 1/2500 (0.04%) chance of being dropped by any enemy in the Riven Hive. Despite being unusable until Plantera has been defeated, it can be dropped at any time during Hardmode. It is used to unlock the Riven Dungeon Chest by right-clicking it while holding this.
