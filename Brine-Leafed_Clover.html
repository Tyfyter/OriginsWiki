Brine-Leafed Clover

The Brine-Leafed Clover is a Hardmode accessory found growing on submerged Peat Moss in the Brine Pool. While equipped, it has a chance to improve the player's odds of obtaining rarer drops from enemies. The clover's sprite will change as leaves are removed whenever it has caused a rare drop to fall for a max amount of 4 uses before it is useless.
