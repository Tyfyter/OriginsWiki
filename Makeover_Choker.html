Makeover Choker

The Makeover Choker is a Hardmode accessory combining Cross Necklace and Mysterious Spray. Equipping it increases the length of invincibility the player has after taking damage and inversely increases their life regeneration as their current life decreases. Like all of Mysterious Spray's variants, the Makeover Choker applies a unique visual effect on the player depending on their current health.

Notes

- Makeover Choker's regeneration rate is inversely logarithmic to the player's current health.
- Makeover Choker has a 1-second ramp up period after taking damage.

Tips

- It is still possible to die with Mysterious Spray and its variants, by recieving quick bursts of damage similar to what it takes to slay a regenerating Defiled enemy.

Trivia

- Although unintentional, it could be assumed the trace amounts of Black Bile present in Mysterious Spray transformed the Cross Necklace into an edgier version of itself.
