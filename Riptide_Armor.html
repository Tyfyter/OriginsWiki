Riptide Armor

Riptide armor is an armor set consisting of the Riptide Helm, Riptide Breastplate and Riptide Greaves. In order to craft the full set the player will need 55 Fallen Stars and 39 Shell Piles.

A full set of Riptide armor provides the following benefits:

- +13 defense
- +10% magic damage
- +6 seconds of breath
- Emits a small aura of light
- +40 mana capacity
- Grants the ability to swim
- Increased movement speed when submerged
Set bonus
- +6% magic damage when submerged or in rain
- Grants the ability to dash, releasing a damaging tidal wave

Tips

- Riptide armor's dash can stop the player from receiving fall damage if used just before landing. It can additionally be used to temporarily maintain altitude.
