Defiled Seeds

Defiled Seeds are seeds which can be planted on Dirt Blocks or Mud Blocks to create Defiled Grass and Defiled Jungle Grass respectively, which will then spread the Defiled in all directions. They drop from the Eye of Cthulhu and can be purchased from the Dryad NPC for 500 each during a Blood Moon in a world with the Defiled. In a world with any other world evil, Defiled Seeds can be purchased from the Dryad if she is spoken to in a Hardmode Graveyard.
