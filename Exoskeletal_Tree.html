Exoskeletal Tree

Exoskeletal Trees are a variant of trees grown on Riven Grass or Riven Jungle Grass blocks found around the Riven Hive. Exoskeletal trees drop Marrowick as a dedicated wood item upon destruction and are chopped down using axes or chainsaws. Additionally, Exoskeletal Trees have a 143/2000 (7.015%) chance to drop a Pawpaw or Periven in normal conditions. During the Halloween event, these chances are slightly reduced to a 34073/500000 (6.8146%). Exoskeletal Trees can be planted anew by planting Acorns on Riven Grass.
