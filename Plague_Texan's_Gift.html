Plague Texan's Gift

Plague Texan's Gift is a semi-functional vanity developer item that can be obtained rarely (6.25%) from Treasure Bags dropped from Hardmode bosses (except Queen Slime's) alongside Tyfyter's set. It is a vanity item made for Tyfyter, the programmer behind Terraria Origins.

It is meant to be equipped in the social slots, but can also provide the player with the Night Owl buff when equipped in an accessory slot.
