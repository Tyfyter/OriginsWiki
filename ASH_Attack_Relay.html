A.S.H. Attack Relay

The A.S.H. Attack Relay is a pre-Hardmode accessory that can be created combining a Comb and Seal of Cinders. The Relay releases a cloud of cinders that trail the player, eventually exploding after a short time. The explosive ashes left behind can home in on nearby enemies.
