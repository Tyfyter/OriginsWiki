Bug Trapper

The Bug Trapper is a pre-Hardmode accessory combining a Shackle with the Primordial Soup. When equipped, the player's life regeneration and defense will be increased depending on the current severity of Torn being applied to them. Visually, the Bug Trapper will generate a swam of flying bugs around the player when equipped.
