Amoeba Toy

The Amoeba Toy is a light pet-summoning item that spawns a floating Mebetroid that follows the player around and emits a dim, teal light. It has a 1/6 (16.67%) chance to be dropped by destroyed Gel Blisters in the Riven Hive. The Meberoid can move through solid objects and liquids unhindered. If it falls far enough behind, it will teleport to the player's location. It is the Riven counterpart to the Corruption's Shadow Orb and the Crimson's Crimson Heart.
