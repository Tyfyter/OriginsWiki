Felnum Einheri

The Felnum Einheri is a pre-Hardmode enemy that spawns on the Floating Islands in Space after Skeletron has been defeated. The einheri will approach the player and jab them with its spear, inflicting Static Shock 2-6 seconds and great knockback. Like the Felnum Guardian, it will attack other non-Felnum-associated enemies.

Trivia


- The einherjar (plural for einheri) were warriors who had died in battle brought to Valhalla by valkyries.
