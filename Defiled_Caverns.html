Defiled Caverns

"The creatures you find in the Wastelands are merely its immune system..."

The Defiled Caverns is a biome that is an underground version of the surface-level Defiled, appearing at and below the Cavern layer. Just like the surface-based Defiled Wastelands, it is mainly composed of Defiled Stone and spreads to adjacent tiles. Although some of the underground veins reach deep enough to access it in pre-Hardmode, most of its exclusive contents will only appear when the world enters Hardmode. After the Wall of Flesh is defeated for the first time, a diagonal stripe of Defiled is generated, creating additional Defiled Caverns.

The Defiled Caverns do not suppress the spawning of other enemies in the underground or Cavern layer. During Hardmode, all enemies killed in the Defiled Caverns have a 20%/36% chance to drop a Soul of Night.

Note: Most characteristics of the Defiled Wastelands also apply to the Defiled Caverns. See the Defiled Wastelands for more extensive information.
