Defiled Mite

The Defiled Mite is a pre-Hardmode enemy that spawns in the Defiled Wastelands at a certain depth. The Defiled Mite has a complex behavior on top of regenerating life and releasing a Defiled Wisp upon its death in Master Mode like any other Defiled enemy. The Mite remains still until it is damaged or a player draws near it, which, paired with the Defiled's desaturated atmosphere, can be hard to spot. Once provoked from damage, it will then proceed to back up before launching itself at the player in a similar manner to Giant Shellies.

Tips

- Using a Hunter Potion or throwing Glowstick where you plan on going before navigating is an effective way to not get surprised by Defiled Mites.

Trivia


- The Defiled Caverns were intended to be accessible in pre-Hardmode and the Mite along with it.
