Defiled Grass

Defiled Grass is a block type found in the Defiled Wastelands. It is the Defiled-equivalent to Grass, and can be converted to other variants with any thrown powder. Defiled Grass can be placed and spread using Defiled Seeds. Given enough time, foliage will start to grow on top of Defiled Grass.

Notes

- Defiled Grass can be quickly removed after being shot by a projectile, but will quickly reclaim that ground within seconds. This is a part of the Defiled Resurgence mechanic where the Defiled cannot be cleansed until all Defiled Hearts are removed from the world.
