Missile Armcannon

The Missile Armcannon is a pre-Hardmode accessory crafted using Destructive Claws and a Gun Glove. Swinging while this is equipped will fire any rockets in the player's inventory from their swinging-hand. Additionally, equipping the Armcannon provides these benefits:

- +30% thrown explosive velocity
- +10% explosive attack speed
- +15% explosive blast radius
- +50% explosive [link knockback | | knockback | | https://terraria.wiki.gg/wiki/Knockback]
- Auto-use on all explosive weapons

Tips

- Like the Gun Glove, the Missile Armcannon benefits from attack speed boosts.
- The Missile Armcannon is effective with the Mine Flayer as it is a swung explosive weapon. Combining their abilities with a Nitro Crate or its upgrades can quickly allow the player to clear their screen so long as they have enough ammunition and protection from their own explosives.
