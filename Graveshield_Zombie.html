Graveshield Zombie

The Graveshield Zombie is a pre-Hardmode enemy that spawns on the surface at night. It has the same defense as the Conehead Zombie, but receives significantly reduced damage whenever it is struck from the front. On the other hand, it will take a normal amount of damage when hit from behind. It has no other behavior from other Zombies.
