Relics

If you are looking for the official page of vanilla relics, click here.

Relics are furniture items dropped by bosses and mini-bosses in Master Mode. They are golden depictions of their respective boss that hover above a pedestal, have a golden particle effect, and emit light when placed close to a light source.
