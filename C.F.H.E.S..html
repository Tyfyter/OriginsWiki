C.F.H.E.S.

The C.F.H.E.S. is a pre-Hardmode accessory crafted using an ACME Crate and Danger Barrel. When equipped, all explosives inflict On Fire!, have a 33% reduced fuse time, an 80% increase to their blast radius and can detect enemies within proximity of their blast.

The C.F.H.E.S. is one of the strongest and latest-game Demolitionist accessories, which makes it a valuable boost to the player's combat effectiveness if obtained early on.
