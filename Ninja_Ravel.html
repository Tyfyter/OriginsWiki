Ninja Ravel

Ninja Ravel is a Hardmode accessory that combines Spider Ravel and Stealth Ravel. Ninja Ravel turns the player into a small sphere, upon double-tapping the Down button, that provides slightly more speed than their normal form and gives a 10% chance to dodge attacks. Ninja Ravel allows the player to latch onto walls and ceilings by pressing the corresponding key whereas Move Left is used to remain attached to a wall directly left of the player or Up is used to remain attached to a ceiling the player can touch. On top of that the player's aggro is reduced by 400 while ravelled.
