Generic Weapon

The Generic Weapon is a pre-Hardmode generic weapon that has a 1/50000 (0.002%) chance to be dropped by any enemy in the game. Using it places a bright green cube where the cursor is on screen that rapidly deals damage to enemies that come in contact with it.

Like other generic weapons, it cannot receive modifiers.
