Impact Dynamite

The Impact Dynamite is an explosive that can be crafted using Dynamite and Peat Moss or purchased from the Demolitionist NPC after selling 150 Peat Moss to him once Hardmode is reached. The Impact Dynamite has an increased velocity from regular dynamite and detonates after colliding with a tile or enemy, or after 5 seconds.
