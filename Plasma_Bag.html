Plasma Bag

Plasma Bag is a pre-Hardmode accessory that has a 1/10 (10%) chance of being dropped by a Purple Slime. While equipped it reduces debuff durations (except for Potion Sickness) by 30%, increases life regeneration by 1, and increases the effectiveness of Bleeding applied to the player.
