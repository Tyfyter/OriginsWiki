Defiled Armor

Defiled armor is an armor set consisting of the Defiled Helmet, Defiled Breastplate and Defiled Greaves. In order to craft the full set the player will need 60 Defiled Bars (180 Lost Ore) and 45 Undead Chunks.

Defiled armor pieces are interchangable with pieces from the Ancient Defiled armor set.

A full set of Defiled armor provides the following benefits:

- +19 defense
- +5% critical strike chance
- +6% damage
Set bonus
- +25% max life
