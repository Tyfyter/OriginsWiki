Last Descendant

The Last Descendant is a Hardmode accessory combining the Star Veil and Locket Necklace. When equipped, the player will have their damage increased by 20%, movement speed increased by 50%, length of invincibility doubled after taking damage. Furthermore, falling star projectiles will fall from the sky onto enemies after taking damage.
