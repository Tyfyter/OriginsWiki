The Cloud Elemental is a pre-Hardmode enemy that spawns in Space nearby Floating Islands. He will move left or right until colliding with a surface, which will prompt him to move in the opposite direction. He will dance while lying on his cloud pointing at the player with a fingergun that which he will charge up to zap the player with lightning. His zaps inflict Static Shock for 4-5 seconds.

Trivia

- The Bestiary entry for the Cloud Elemental: "A chill dude just vibing on his cloud. He doesn't mind your presence, he just doesn't know that lightning kills Terrarians."
- The Cloud Elemental can be seen wearing Felnum Shock Glasses himself.
