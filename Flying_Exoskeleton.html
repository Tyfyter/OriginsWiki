Flying Exoskeleton

The Flying Exoskeleton is a pre-Hardmode enemy that only spawns by being summoned by the World Cracker from an Amniotic Sac or as its armor is destroyed. Flying Exoskeletons pursue the player at high speeds and circle around after passing the player for another attack. They deal low damage and inflict little Riven Assimilation but the assimilation they apply can add up quickly as they strike the player repeatedly.

Notes

- Flying Exoskeletons birthed from Amniotic Sacs have half their usual max life.
